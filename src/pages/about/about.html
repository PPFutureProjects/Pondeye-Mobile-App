About Thanks for testing out Pondeye! What is Pondeye?

A fitness journal platform for logging and sharing your fitness goals with others.

 Why Pondeye?

Sharing the experience of achieving your goals inspires others to achieve theirs.

Making your goals visible creates a sense of accountability needed in achieving daring goals.

 How Pondeye Works Create New Entry

Press below to create new goals and milestones.

Goals are things you want to accomplish. Milestones are checkpoints within the goals.

Goal: "Run a half marathon on the 22nd of April".

Milestones: "Run the 2 miles hill near my house tomorrow"

Upon Completion of goal or milestone, REMEMBER to mark it as completed in your profile page. If you don't do this prior to the completion time, it will be marked as failed (we don't want that!).

Before and After Pictures

Pondeye is about sharing your experiences with others. Hence, you can capture before and after pictures that reflect the progress of your goals.

Click this button to do so

Ponds (Communities)

Ponds are interest and motivation based groups that you can jion or create.

You see how to achieve your goals by seeing how others with similar interest and motivation accomplish theirs.

 Create and Find Ponds

Simply use the search bar to find a pond based on name or interest.

Example of Pond Entry:

Name of Pond: Fitness for profesional women.

Purpose: A pond for busy professional women interested in staying in shape.

Someone can find this pond by using these key words: "in shape", "professional", "women" etc.

 Vouch

By clicking the vouch button you are telling a user that I believe in you and I am realistic about your chances of achieving this milestone.

If a user completes a goal you vouched for, it adds more points to that user's ranking and increases your credibility.

Be thoughtful with your vouch. If a user deosn't complete the tasks it affects your vouch credibility.

Your vouch credibility is like your word. The less accurate you are at vouching correctly, the less impact your vouch has on user's goals
